Cailin Carrier

Hi! I'm Cailin,

I'm an Australian who came to Canada on a working holiday for a year... Three years ago.

I left my job as a Paramedic in Tasmania, Australia to travel aroudn Canada fora year and immediately fell in love with Whistler, and I just don't want to leave!

Living in the mountains, riding my mountain bike every chance I get and drawing in my down time, I feel incredibly lucky

I love recreating people's favourite photographs, particularly portraits and animals, but am always open to trying new ideas and artworks!

Please send me an email for any enquiries.
